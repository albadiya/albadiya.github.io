SKS INTERNATIONAL TRANSPORT

S K S International Transport LLC is your reliable transportation partner.

We are a fast-moving diversified general transportation company based in Abu Dhabi, UAE. We specialize in trucking, heavy construction equipment and machinery rental of different types like excavators, flatbed and lowbed trailers, cranes, buses, sewerage tankers, water tankers, shovels, pick-ups, boom loaders, JCBs and more.


We do not just rent the equipment but provide a good quality service, thorough follow up and commitment to our clients and partners. Our daily nonstop learning process and continuously growing fleet of land transport (trucks) and heavy construction equipment make us stand out in this market.
