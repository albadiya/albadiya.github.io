CONSTRUCTION










Construction & Development

Construction is a general term meaning the art and science to form objects, systems, or organizations, and comes from Latin constructio and Old French construction. To construct is the verb: the act of building, and the noun is construction:.

General Contractor SKS Contracting company’s core values are to build quality construction projects according to their original specifications, on time and in budget. Architectural craftsmanship, engineering, integrity, honesty, and quality workmanship are what they have built their reputation. Customer satisfaction drives every aspect of their commercial contractor business. These time-honored qualities are incorporated into every project built by SKS Construction company.
While their values are traditional and their experience vast, they remain on the forefront of technology as it applies to the commercial construction trade. They maintain sophisticated information systems that are user friendly, allowing the owner to keep updated with all phases of construction from any place in the world with Internet access. Staying on the leading edge of our industry is important to the company; they spend more money on employee education, computer hardware and software than they do on advertising and entertainment.
Our vision is to become the customer’s first choice in project execution by seeking continual improvement to our corporate system.
Our dedicated team will strive to bring growth to the community while assisting its clients in achieving their visions
SKS CONTRACTING COMPANY. mission statement is to sustain our leadership in providing excellence in the field of construction and designing; while focusing on customers values and requirements to deliver our projects at a minimum time and cost with exceptional workmanship, service response and professionalism within a safe work environment.
Commitment:
SKS CONTRACTING COMPANY is committed in establishing quality objectives in order to fulfill the needs and satisfaction of our clients, by delivering products in a healthy and safe manner as per legal requirements, contractual specifications and within the most economical solution. SKS CONTRACTING COMPANY strives in understanding and helping the clients in order to achieve their goals.
Teamwork:
Employees are the roots of any company’s success. This is why we aim to ensure a rewarding environment in order to help and encourage our employees to reach their potential. Our workforce represents a united team which generates mutual respect, motivation and hard work in order to reach its target.
Integrity:
We act with honesty, fairness, respect and safety. Our culture of integrity strengthens relationships among clients, partners and employees. It strives to create, follow and encourage the highest ethical standards within and beyond our company.
